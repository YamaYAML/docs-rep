# The Tavern
 
A guild of fiery ambition and unwavering determination emerges, vibrant in its youth yet unyielding in its strength. With resolute steps, we forge ahead, conquering the treacherous realms of end-game content, while our bonds with social and leveling members remain unbreakable.

## What is this?

This platform will contain all guild rules, guidelines and resources. It will act as a single-source of thruth.
